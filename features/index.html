Memoix - 2026

Capabilities.

A relational archive for the kitchen. Memoix replaces generic templates with specialized models for formats that don't fit standard structures.

The Model

Flexible Recipe Architecture

Memoix handles smoking pit notes, pizza compositions, and modernist process logs alongside conventional recipes. Entries can be partial, technical, or format-specific without needing to conform.

Pit NotesModernist TechniquesCellar Logs

Ingestion

Import & Normalisation

OCR for physical books, URL parsing for web recipes, and deep-link imports. Imported recipes have fractions, units and notation are standardised automatically for consistency.

Optional AI-assisted import is available via user-provided API keys for particularly difficult formats.

The Line

Side-by-Side Cooking

Ingredients and directions scroll independently in Side-by-Side mode. Long-press any timed direction to trigger a persistent background timer that continues even if the app isn't focused.

Long press any ingredient to view flavour profiles and substitutions when something is missing.

Ingredient Scaling

Long-press serving sizes to scale quantities in-session. Original measurements remain intact in the database.

Recipe Sharing

Share recipes via QR codes, text messages, or email. Including offline transfer between devices.

Step-Linked Images

Gallery photos can be pinned to specific steps, allowing for visual checks on texture or colour without losing your place.

Kitchen Tools

Measurement converters, recipe comparison, and an auto-saved scratch pad for mid-process notes. Recipes can be compared, reduced, and combined into new drafts.
