Memoix - 2026

Intent.

Memoix is a tool for people who cook, taste, and adjust. It keeps recipes, notes, and working records usable during cooking.

Core Values

  • Clarity over presentation
  • Flexibility over structure
  • Legibility over density
  • Use over optimization

The Anti-Social

Memoix is not a social platform, a learning course, or a productivity game. It stays out of the way and no account is required.

This is by design, not oversight.

Visual Logic

Asymmetry is intentional. Structure adapts to the type of work. Recipes are written quickly, adjusted often, and remembered imperfectly.

Tone

Confident. Professional in use. Opinionated when it matters. Measured throughout.

Built with salt.
